## 💼 PRO TIPS FOR THIS GIG

- Restaurant owners respond well to visual demos — record a 60-second app walkthrough video showing food ordering in action.
- Target local restaurant owners not just globally — use keywords like "restaurant app for small business".
- Mention "QR code menu" prominently — this became hugely popular post-COVID and remains in high demand.
- Offer a "starter menu" package for small cafés or single-location restaurants at a lower price point to attract first-time tech buyers.
- Common buyer persona: restaurant owner aged 30-50, not very technical, willing to pay well for a solution that "just works".
