# REFERENCE IMAGE PROMPT — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER

---

## Reference Image Generation Prompt

```
Full-body character reference sheet of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier — a professional private military contractor enemy infantry — from the 2.5D isometric dieselpunk game IRON BREACH.

CAMERA: Isometric 2.5D view. 30-degree pitch. Soldier faces south (toward viewer), front-diagonal angle.

BACKGROUND: Flat neutral dark grey (#1A1A1A). No environment. Character casts soft drop shadow below feet.

ART STYLE: Semi-realistic painterly sprite style — same art direction as Sergeant Kael Dros and all characters in IRON BREACH. Crisp 2px black outline. Readable at 64×64 px. NOT pixel art. NOT anime. NOT cartoon. Painterly, detailed, but sprite-legible. The character must read as clean, professional, and corporate — the visual opposite of the Reclaimer Militia Soldier.

HEIGHT AND BUILD: Same height and general build as Kael Dros (~1.85m equivalent, 3-tile sprite height). Athletic and lean — armored but not bulky. Upright, controlled posture.

HEAD / HELMET:
- Full-face ballistic helmet: smooth modern composite shell, matte black (#1A1A1A). No surface texture or visible wear — maintained. Clean.
- Integrated visor covering the full face: tinted dark grey (#2A2A30), no cracks, no reflections visible through the visor (opaque from the outside). The visor is a single smooth surface.
- Left side of helmet: SENTINEL logo — a stylized "S" in a clean thin-line white mark (#E0E0E0). Small, approximately the size of a thumbnail. Precise, corporate design.
- Right side of helmet: a small camera module (surveillance). Black rectangular box, 2cm, clipped to the helmet rail. Tiny lens circle at front.
- Earpiece in right ear: barely visible beneath the helmet — a small black bud.

TORSO:
- Close-fitting black tactical jacket (#1E1E22), subtle fabric texture (ripstop). No chest rig worn externally. Front panel slightly differs in sheen from the rest of the jacket — suggesting internal magazine pockets built into the jacket panels (not exaggerated, just subtle panel lines).
- PLATE CARRIER over the jacket: this is the KEY visual element.
  - Front plate: MATTE WHITE (#DCDCDC) — a large rectangular chest plate covering the torso front from collarbone to waist. This white element must be prominent and clearly readable.
  - Straps: black (#1A1A1A), crossing from the shoulders down to the plate sides. Clean, flat, no buckle bulk.
  - SENTINEL logo on the right chest area of the plate carrier: black-on-white, slightly subdued — the familiar "S" logo in corporate clean print. Readable at medium size.
- Left wrist: slim black tactical terminal strapped to the wrist — a small rectangular device with a minimal screen face (dark screen, no glow at rest).

ARMS:
- Both arms: fitted black tactical sleeves from shoulder to wrist.
- Both hands: full-finger black tactical gloves. No skin visible anywhere.
- Right hand: holds compact assault carbine.

ASSAULT CARBINE:
- Short barrel, folding/folded stock at the rear.
- Black suppressor attached to muzzle.
- Clean matte black finish throughout — this is a maintained weapon, not a worn one.
- Small white serial number strip on the receiver side: tiny white text, barely readable at small scale. Present for detail fidelity.
- No visible scope — red-dot sight (tiny circle sight on top of receiver — minimal).

LEGS:
- Black tactical trousers (#1A1A22), clean and fitted.
- KNEE PADS: matte white (#DCDCDC), matching the plate carrier color. Both knees. This white color match between chest plate and knee pads is intentional — it is a SENTINEL design identity mark.
- Right thigh: black holster with black sidearm in it. Clean, secure.
- Boots: ankle-high modern tactical boots (#141414), clean black, modern design.

OVERALL IMPRESSION:
- The SENTINEL Soldier reads as: expensive, professional, corporate.
- Black and white color scheme is dominant — the only warm tones are the matte white plate and knee pads. Everything else is near-black.
- No improvisation. No worn edges. No mismatched kit. Everything is issued and maintained.
- Visual contrast to Kael: Kael is warm olive/amber, worn. SENTINEL is cold black/white, clean.
- Visual contrast to Reclaimer: Reclaimer is warm brown chaos. SENTINEL is cold black order.

STANCE: Reference/neutral pose. Upright controlled standing. Assault carbine in right hand, low-ready (barrel down). Left arm at side. Feet shoulder-width apart — not aggressive, but ready.

LIGHTING: Soft top-left directional. Baked into sprite. Same style as all other character reference sheets in this project.

OUTPUT: Single soldier, centered, full body head to boot, on dark background. Sprite reference sheet format. This character must be immediately readable as a different faction from Kael and from Reclaimers at a glance — the white plate carrier is the key differentiator.
```
