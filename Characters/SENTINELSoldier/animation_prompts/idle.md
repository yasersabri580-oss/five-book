# ANIMATION PROMPTS — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER — IDLE

---

## Instructions for All Idle Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the direction changes. Costume, helmet, white plate carrier, white knee pads, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Idle is looping. First and last frames must match silhouette for seamless loop.
- SENTINEL Soldiers do not fidget. Their idle is alert, controlled, and upright. Head performs a slow scan (10–15° sweep) over the loop. Body remains still — weight evenly distributed, no weight shift.
- Carbine in low-ready throughout idle — both hands on weapon, barrel angled down-forward, finger alongside guard (not on trigger).
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

## IDLE — FACING DOWN (South, toward camera)

```
Isometric 2.5D idle animation frame of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, facing south (toward the viewer). Front-diagonal view.

Preserve exactly from reference: matte black full-face ballistic helmet (SENTINEL logo on left side, camera module on right), white matte plate carrier (prominent front chest plate #DCDCDC), black tactical jacket and trousers, white knee pads (#DCDCDC), compact suppressed carbine, full-finger black gloves, left wrist terminal. Do NOT change any visual element.

Pose: Upright at-rest. Feet shoulder-width apart, evenly weighted (no lean). Both hands on carbine — barrel angled down-forward, finger straight alongside guard. Head facing south with subtle 10° tilt to the right (scanning motion — one phase of the scan loop). Body completely vertical — no forward lean. White plate carrier front fully visible and prominent. The read is: professional, disciplined, controlled.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline, readable at 64×64 px display size.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
```
