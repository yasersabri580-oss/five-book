# ANIMATION PROMPTS — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER — DEATH

---

## Instructions for All Death Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the fall direction changes. Costume, helmet, white plate carrier, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Death animation: 4-frame sequence — stagger → buckle → collapse → final rest (held, no loop).
- SENTINEL death is less dramatic than Reclaimer death. Their collapse is controlled — a rigid body falling, not a thrashing impact. Even in death, their bodies read as disciplined.
- The white plate carrier must be visible in the final rest frame (from the facing direction that would show the front).
- Carbine falls near or with the body — not dropped violently.
- Final rest frame is permanently held on screen.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

---

## DEATH — FALL FORWARD (Hit from rear / behind)

```
Isometric 2.5D death animation final rest frame of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, fallen forward (face-down).

Preserve exactly from reference: matte black helmet, white plate carrier (back plate visible from this face-down perspective — dark back plate #2A2A2A), black jacket, black trousers, white knee pads.

Final pose: Face-down collapse. Body oriented horizontally across the isometric tile. Matte black helmet pressed face-down (visor toward ground — top of helmet and rear of helmet visible). Both arms at sides — not dramatically spread (less dramatic collapse than Reclaimer). Carbine fallen parallel to the body on the right side. Dark back plate of plate carrier visible (the character is face-down, so back faces up). White knee pads visible from rear/above. Body completely still. Rigid collapse — straight lines.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Note: Held final frame — completely still.
```
