# ANIMATION PROMPTS — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER — HURT

---

## Instructions for All Hurt Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the hit-reaction pose changes. Costume, helmet, white plate carrier, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Hurt is a short non-looping stagger reaction: 2 frames (impact → controlled recovery start).
- SENTINEL Soldiers stagger LESS than Reclaimers or civilians. They are disciplined — their hurt reads as controlled damage response, not wild impact. Torso lean: maximum ~12° (vs Reclaimer ~25°, vs Kael ~20°).
- They never drop the carbine — it dips but stays in hand.
- No helmet change — visor remains intact. No new damage.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

## HURT — FACING DOWN (South, hit from front)

```
Isometric 2.5D hurt-reaction frame of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, hit from the south (front impact). Front-diagonal view.

Preserve exactly from reference: matte black helmet, white plate carrier (front plate #DCDCDC), black jacket, trousers, white knee pads, carbine. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Impact from front — controlled rearward rock. Torso lean: 12° backward (much less than Reclaimer). Right foot slides one half-step back to absorb impact. Carbine drops from low-ready to chest level (arms absorb the jolt) — carbine still held in both hands. Left arm comes forward slightly in a controlled defensive reflex. Head snaps back ~5°. Visor intact. Body language reads as: "took a hit, processing, recovering." Never flailing, never wild.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
```
