# ANIMATION PROMPTS — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER — RUN

---

## Instructions for All Run Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only direction and dynamic pose change. Costume, helmet, white plate carrier, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Run cycle: 6-frame looping. Controlled sprint — forward lean only ~10°. Much less lean than Reclaimer or Kael.
- Carbine held at chest height in both hands during sprint — raised into a ready carry, not dangling loose. Both hands remain on weapon. This is a trained soldier's sprint, not a panicked run.
- Legs show full stride extension — one foot always airborne. Knees drive forward efficiently.
- White plate carrier front must remain readable during all forward-facing run directions.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

## RUN — FACING DOWN (South)

```
Isometric 2.5D sprint animation frame of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, sprinting south (toward the viewer). Peak right-foot stride.

Preserve exactly from reference: matte black full-face helmet, white plate carrier (front plate #DCDCDC prominent), black jacket, black trousers, white knee pads, compact suppressed carbine. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Controlled sprint south. Forward lean ~10° (disciplined — not an all-out charge lean). Right foot extended forward (peak stride, heel about to strike). Left foot kicked back and up — clear airborne phase. Both hands on carbine, weapon raised to chest height and held close to body — ready carry position (not aimed, not dangling). Very controlled arm movement — no wild swing. Head forward and slightly down in sprint focus. White plate carrier front compressed slightly from forward lean but still prominent and readable.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline, dynamic energy.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
```
