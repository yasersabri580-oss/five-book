# ANIMATION PROMPTS — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER — WALK

---

## Instructions for All Walk Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only direction and limb positions change. Costume, helmet, white plate carrier, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Walk cycle: 6-frame looping. Military precision gait — heel-to-toe, very controlled arm swing, minimal torso rotation.
- SENTINEL Soldiers walk UPRIGHT — no forward lean. This distinguishes them from Kael (slightly hunched) and Reclaimers (aggressive forward lean).
- Carbine in low-ready throughout walk — both hands on weapon, barrel angled down-forward. The carbine does NOT swing freely at their side.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

---

## WALK — FACING DOWN (South)

```
Isometric 2.5D walk animation frame (mid-stride) of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, walking south (toward the viewer).

Preserve exactly from reference: matte black full-face helmet (SENTINEL logo left, camera module right), white matte plate carrier (front plate #DCDCDC prominent), black jacket, black trousers, white knee pads, compact suppressed carbine, black gloves, left wrist terminal. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Mid-stride south. Right foot extended forward — heel-to-toe plant. Left foot at push-off (toe contact only). Torso upright — MINIMAL rotation (this is a disciplined march-walk, not a casual stride). Both hands on carbine throughout — very controlled arm movement. Carbine stays at low-ready: barrel down-forward, not swinging. Right leg extended, white right knee pad prominent. Head forward, visor facing south. White plate carrier front fully visible.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline, readable at small display size.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
```
