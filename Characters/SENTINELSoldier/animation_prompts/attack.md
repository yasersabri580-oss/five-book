# ANIMATION PROMPTS — SENTINEL CORPORATE SOLDIER — ATTACK

---

## Instructions for All Attack Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the attack pose and direction change. Costume, helmet, white plate carrier, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- SENTINEL Soldiers fire with aimed discipline — carbine raised to shoulder, proper two-hand grip. This is NOT hip-fire.
- Firing stance: slight crouch (~10°), feet shoulder-width apart, controlled weight distribution.
- Muzzle flash is a separate engine particle — do NOT bake into sprite.
- Suppressor on carbine tip must always be visible in aim direction (foreshortened when pointing at viewer).
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

---

## ATTACK — CARBINE AIMED FIRE — FACING DOWN (South)

```
Isometric 2.5D aimed-fire animation frame of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, shooting south (toward viewer). Two-handed aimed fire stance.

Preserve exactly from reference: matte black helmet, white plate carrier (front plate prominent #DCDCDC), black jacket, black trousers, white knee pads, compact suppressed carbine. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Raised carbine to right shoulder — professional aim stance. Right hand on grip (trigger finger extended over guard — aim discipline). Left hand supporting forend. Carbine elevated to shoulder height. Barrel + suppressor tip pointing directly at viewer (foreshortened suppressor visible at barrel tip). Slight crouch (~10°) to lower center of gravity — controlled weight distribution, feet shoulder-width. Very minor recoil suggestion: barrel 3° above eye line at recoil peak. Helmet visor angled toward barrel line. White plate carrier front FULLY VISIBLE — this is the key faction identification moment.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
Note: Muzzle flash is engine particle at suppressor tip. Suppressor visible as a cylindrical extension at barrel end.
```

# ATTACK — WRIST TERMINAL CALL (Reinforcement Signal)

## Instructions
2 directions (left and right facing — soldier holds up left wrist toward viewer side). Non-looping. 2-frame sequence: raise wrist → press terminal button.

---

## WRIST CALL — FACING LEFT (Left wrist toward viewer)

```
Isometric 2.5D wrist-terminal call animation frame of a SENTINEL Corporate Soldier, activating left wrist terminal. Facing left (west).

Preserve exactly from reference: all costume elements locked. Left wrist terminal is a small black rectangular device strapped to left wrist, dark screen face.

Pose: Soldier has stopped moving. Left arm raised to chest height — forearm horizontal, left wrist terminal visible and prominent at wrist (facing viewer, screen side toward viewer). Right hand presses the terminal with index finger extended and depressing the screen surface. Carbine slung diagonally across the body or tucked under the right arm for this brief action. Head looking down at the terminal — deliberate, calm, professional. This is a trained protocol, not a panicked call.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Note: A very faint white pulse glow at the terminal screen is an engine particle effect at terminal screen position — do not bake into sprite.
```
