# REFERENCE IMAGE PROMPT — RINA

---

## Purpose
This prompt generates the **canonical reference image** for Rina.
All portrait and cutscene art must reference this image for visual identity consistency.

---

## Reference Image Generation Prompt

```
Full-body and portrait reference sheet of Rina, a female character in a 2.5D isometric dieselpunk military game. She is a civilian-turned-resistance-strategist, not a soldier.

CAMERA: Dual-view reference sheet. Left panel: isometric 2.5D full body (south-facing, 30-degree pitch, same camera as the game). Right panel: close-up three-quarter portrait (slight left-facing, from chest up). Background on both panels: flat dark near-black (#121212). No environment.

ART STYLE: Same semi-realistic painterly sprite style as Sergeant Kael Dros in this project. Crisp 2px black outline. Readable at 128×128 px for the portrait. NOT pixel art. NOT anime. NOT cartoon. NOT full 3D. Painterly, detailed but sprite-legible.

FACE:
- Sharp angular features, high cheekbones, defined jawline
- Warm olive-brown skin (#7A5A3A)
- Dark near-black brown eyes (#2A1A10) — alert, scanning, slightly narrowed
- Small scar along the LEFT jawline — a pale line (#8A6A4A), subtle, old, 2cm in length, slightly below the cheekbone on the left jaw. IMPORTANT: subtle, not dramatic.
- No makeup
- Short hair: close-cropped on the sides and back, slightly longer (3–4cm) on top, unstyled, slight natural texture. Color: dark near-black brown (#1A1008).

CLOTHING:
- Dark charcoal grey fitted jacket (#2E2E2E), standing collar slightly worn at edges, front zipper zipped to about 3/4 of the way up. Jacket fits close to the body — not baggy.
- Dark navy crew-neck undershirt (#1A2030) visible at the collar edge above the zipper.
- Left upper arm: Rift faction patch — black oval background, white jagged fracture/lightning bolt line (#FFFFFF). Small, approximately the size of a thumb. Stitched on, slightly worn at edges.

HEADSET:
- RIGHT ear: over-ear tactical headset. Matte black (#1A1A1A), simple civilian design, padded ear cup. A short mic arm extends from the ear cup forward toward her right cheek. The mic head is a small capsule at the end of the arm.
- LEFT ear: small black in-ear earpiece. Barely visible.

GLOVES:
- Dark grey fingerless gloves (#303030), worn at the knuckle edge of the fingers. Covers palm and back of hand, fingers exposed from the second knuckle.

FULL BODY (left panel):
- Standing in a neutral controlled posture. Arms slightly away from body. No weapon visible (Rina is rear-command, Chapter 1). Hands relaxed — not fists, not raised.
- Left shoulder: worn leather map case strap (#7A6040) crossing diagonally from left shoulder to right hip. The case itself visible at right hip — worn leather flap, small.
- Feet: dark tactical boots (#1A1A1A), ankle-height, clean compared to Kael's boots.

PORTRAIT (right panel):
- Tight framing: chest to crown of head. Slight left-facing (character looks slightly to the right of frame — a standard three-quarter portrait convention). Eyes slightly narrowed, alert but not hostile.
- This portrait must be clear and readable at 128×128 px. Face must fill at least 50% of the portrait frame.
- Expression: neutral intelligence. This is her default HUD portrait — not smiling, not grimacing, simply present and alert.

PROPORTIONS: 1:7 head-to-body ratio. Lean build. No exaggerated body proportions. Realistic female adult proportions.

LIGHTING: Soft top-left directional. Baked into sprite — clean sprite lighting, no dramatic shadows. Slight rim light on right edge (consistent with Kael's reference sheet lighting style for visual cohesion).

OUTPUT: Two-panel reference sheet. Left panel: full body isometric. Right panel: portrait three-quarter. Both on same dark background. Character is visually cohesive and production-ready. This is a sprite reference sheet, not concept art.
```
