# ANIMATION PROMPTS — FORGE INFANTRY DRONE — DEATH

---

## Instructions for All Death Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the fall direction changes. Body design, arm assignments, and all mechanical details are strictly locked.**
- Death animation: 4-frame sequence — stagger → buckle → collapse → final rest (held, no loop).
- Drone death is a mechanical failure — the legs buckle and the body drops. No thrashing, no dramatic spread. The machine simply stops.
- Sensor eyes go COMPLETELY DARK (#1A1A1A — no glow) at death. This must be reflected in the final rest frame.
- The body collapses in a heap — not a spread-eagle fall. Reverse-knee legs fold under or splay outward from the buckle.
- Cannon arm may separate slightly from its default position due to collapse impact.
- Final rest frame is permanently held on screen. No animation loop.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

## DEATH — COLLAPSE FORWARD (Hit from rear)

```
Isometric 2.5D death animation final rest frame of a FORGE Infantry Drone, collapsed forward. Fell forward from a rear impact.

Preserve exactly from reference: matte gunmetal grey chassis, flat black panel seams, FORGE logo on right torso panel, reverse-knee legs (now folded/buckled beneath the body), cannon arm (right arm) and claw arm (left arm). CRITICAL: Sensor eyes = completely dark (#1A1A1A). No cyan glow anywhere on the sensor module area.

Final pose: Body collapsed forward — front of chassis against the ground in isometric view. Reverse-knee legs buckled outward or folded underneath the body at unnatural angles (machine structural failure). The legs are NOT neatly arranged — they have buckled at the knee joints and splayed. Cannon arm beneath or alongside the body, barrel angled against the ground. Claw arm splayed to one side. FORGE logo on right torso panel now faces the ground (partially visible from the side). Body is compact and low — the machine is a crumpled heap, not a spread-out fall. Sensor eyes DARK.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
Note: Held final frame — no loop.
```
