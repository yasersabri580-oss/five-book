# ANIMATION PROMPTS — KAEL DROS — HURT

---

## Instructions for All Hurt Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the hit-reaction pose changes. Costume and proportions are locked.**
- Hurt is a short non-looping stagger reaction: 3 frames (impact → max stagger → recovery start).
- The character staggers backward from the direction of hit. Use the facing direction to determine the stagger lean.
- Visor should remain intact — no damage changes to the visor in hurt frames.
- Mechanical arm raises defensively in most hurt poses — instinctive shield gesture.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

---

## HURT — FACING DOWN (South, hit from the south / front)

```
Isometric 2.5D hurt-reaction animation frame (peak stagger) of Sergeant Kael Dros, hit from the front while facing south.

Preserve exactly from reference: cracked amber visor helmet, gunmetal mechanical left arm, canvas chest rig, dark cargo trousers, heavy boots. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Hit from front — character staggers backward (northward lean). Head snapped slightly back. Shoulders pulled back. Mechanical left arm raises to chest/face height in defensive reflex — elbow bent, forearm up (partial guard). Right hand grips rifle loosely — arm flung slightly back from impact. Knees buckled slightly. Right foot slides back one pace (impact rearward momentum). Torso leans backward ~20°.

Expression: Visor provides no expression — body language carries the pain read.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
```
