# ANIMATION PROMPTS — KAEL DROS — WALK

---

## Instructions for All Walk Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the direction and limb positions change per frame. Costume, proportions, silhouette, and visor design are locked.**
- Walk cycle: 6-frame minimum looping cycle. Frame 1 and Frame 6 must match silhouette for seamless loop.
- Rifle is held in **low-ready** during walk — barrel slightly elevated, right hand on grip, left (mechanical) hand may loosely rest under barrel mid-stride.
- Walk pace: measured, alert. Not leisurely. Not sprinting. Combat-ready walk.
- Foot strike pattern: heel-to-toe, slight knee bend at mid-stride.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.
