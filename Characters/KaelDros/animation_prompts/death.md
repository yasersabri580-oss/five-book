# ANIMATION PROMPTS — KAEL DROS — DEATH

---

## Instructions for All Death Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the fall direction changes. Costume and proportions are locked.**
- Death animation: 5-frame sequence — stagger → buckle → collapse → ground impact → final rest pose (held, no loop).
- Final rest frame is the **permanent frame** that stays on screen until respawn/mission fail.
- Show the character clearly defeated — no ambiguity. The final frame must read as death, not unconsciousness.
- Visor is intact — no shattering or cracking beyond existing crack (that is a pre-existing design feature).
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

---

## DEATH — FALL FORWARD (Hit from the North / Behind)

```
Isometric 2.5D death animation final rest frame of Sergeant Kael Dros, fallen forward. Character was hit from behind and collapsed forward onto the ground.

Preserve exactly from reference: cracked amber visor helmet, gunmetal mechanical left arm, canvas chest rig, cargo trousers, heavy boots. Do NOT change any costume element.

Final pose: Character lying face-down on the ground in isometric view. Body oriented horizontally across the isometric tile. Helmet face-down (visor toward ground, rear of helmet visible). Rifle on ground to the right of the body, separated from hand. Boots toes-down, heels facing viewer. Chest rig visible from side. No blood required — visual indicators of defeat are body position, stillness, and powered-down arm glow.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
Note: This is the held final frame — no animation loop. Completely still.
```
