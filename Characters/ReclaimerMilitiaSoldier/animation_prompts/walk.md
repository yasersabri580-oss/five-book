# ANIMATION PROMPTS — RECLAIMER MILITIA SOLDIER — WALK

---

## Instructions for All Walk Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only direction and limb positions change. Costume, helmet, rubber shoulder armor, bandoliers, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Walk cycle: 6-frame looping. Heavier, stomping gait compared to Kael and SENTINEL. More torso sway. This is a stocky, physical fighter.
- Rifle is carried at side — barrel down, one-handed (right hand), swinging somewhat freely. NOT in low-ready. This is the most visible behavioral contrast with SENTINEL soldiers.
- More torso rotation than SENTINEL (less disciplined stride).
- Rubber shoulder armor on left bounces slightly with stride rhythm.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.
