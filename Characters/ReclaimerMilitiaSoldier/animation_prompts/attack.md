# ANIMATION PROMPTS — RECLAIMER MILITIA SOLDIER — ATTACK

---

## Instructions for All Attack Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the attack pose and direction change. Costume, helmet, rubber shoulder armor, bandoliers, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Reclaimers fire from the hip or in a loose raised stance — NOT disciplined shoulder-aim like SENTINEL. The contrast must be clear.
- Hip-fire stance: both hands on rifle, raised to approximately chest-to-waist height, barrel pointing in target direction, slightly unstable looking.
- Recoil is more visible than SENTINEL — the Reclaimer takes harder recoil hits due to loose grip and stance.
- Molotov throw: separate attack set.
- Muzzle flash: engine particle — do NOT bake into sprite.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

## ATTACK — RIFLE FIRE (HIP) — FACING DOWN (South)

```
Isometric 2.5D rifle-fire animation frame of a Reclaimer Militia Soldier, firing south (toward viewer). Hip-fire / loose raised stance.

Preserve exactly from reference: brown spray-painted helmet, olive jacket, rubber left shoulder armor, bandoliers across chest, AK-variant rifle (both hands), fingerless right glove, left forearm tattoo, cargo trousers, right boot duct tape. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Hip-fire or low-raised fire stance. Rifle held at approximately chest-waist height — NOT pressed to shoulder. Right hand on grip (trigger), left hand on forend or wooden stock area. Barrel points south at viewer (foreshortened muzzle visible). Stance is slightly spread and unstable-looking — one foot slightly forward. Recoil suggestion: barrel kicked upward-left from the burst — 8–10° rise (more than SENTINEL's 3°). Left shoulder armor jolted slightly from recoil. This looks aggressive but imprecise — classic Reclaimer fire discipline.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
Note: Muzzle flash is engine particle at barrel tip.
```

# ATTACK — MOLOTOV THROW

## Instructions
4 cardinal directions. Non-looping. 3-frame sequence: grip bottle → wind-up → overhand throw release.
Molotov: glass bottle with rag wick, slight liquid visible (flame on wick is engine particle — do not bake).

---

## MOLOTOV THROW — FACING DOWN (South)

```
Isometric 2.5D molotov throw release frame of a Reclaimer Militia Soldier, throwing south (toward viewer).

Preserve exactly from reference: helmet, jacket, shoulder armor, bandoliers. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Right arm fully extended overhead in overhand throw arc — hand open, molotov just released (glass bottle with rag wick visible at fingertip, launching upward-forward). Left hand at side or slightly forward for balance. Torso leans back slightly from throw counterbalance. Right foot planted (power base). Aggressive, committed throw motion — this is a full-force throw, not a gentle lob. Rag wick on bottle visible (flame is engine particle). Rifle slung or dropped behind body for this action.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
```
