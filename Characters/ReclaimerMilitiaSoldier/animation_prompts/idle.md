# ANIMATION PROMPTS — RECLAIMER MILITIA SOLDIER — IDLE

---

## Instructions for All Idle Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the direction changes. Helmet, jacket, rubber shoulder armor, bandoliers, weapon, and mismatched boots are strictly locked.**
- Idle is looping. First and last frames must match silhouette.
- Reclaimers do NOT stand still well. Their idle is restless and aggressive — weight shift every loop, occasional head turn. They lean, they shift, they look like they want to move.
- Rifle is carried casually — held loosely in one hand at side (barrel down), not in low-ready. This is the most visible contrast with SENTINEL's disciplined idle.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.
