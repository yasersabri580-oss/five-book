# ANIMATION PROMPTS — RECLAIMER MILITIA SOLDIER — HURT

---

## Instructions for All Hurt Prompts
- Use the canonical reference image (see `reference_prompt.md`) as the visual source of truth.
- **Only the hit-reaction pose changes. Costume, helmet, rubber shoulder armor, bandoliers, and weapon are strictly locked.**
- Hurt: 2–3 frames, non-looping. More dramatic than SENTINEL hurt — Reclaimers take hits expressively. Torso lean ~25°. Arms fling more. More wild than controlled.
- They may briefly lose rifle grip during hurt but regain it in recovery frame.
- Rubber shoulder armor bounces dramatically on impact.
- Sprite size: 192×192 px per frame. Isometric 2.5D. 30-degree pitch.

---

## HURT — FACING DOWN (South, hit from front)

```
Isometric 2.5D hurt-reaction frame of a Reclaimer Militia Soldier, hit from the south (front). Front-diagonal view.

Preserve exactly from reference: brown spray-painted helmet, olive jacket, rubber left shoulder armor, bandoliers, AK-variant rifle, fingerless glove, left forearm tattoo, cargo trousers, duct tape boot. Do NOT change any costume element.

Pose: Dramatic impact from front — strong rearward rock. Torso thrown backward ~25° (significantly more than SENTINEL's 12°). Head snaps back hard. Right arm (rifle arm) flung backward — rifle swings back and up, almost leaving the hand (loose grip). Left arm thrown outward and slightly back. Left shoulder armor jolts dramatically upward from impact. Both knees buckled. Right foot slides back. The read is: visceral hit, wild response, survival instinct — not professional damage management.

Background: Transparent PNG.
Art style: Semi-realistic painterly sprite, 2px black outline.
Lighting: Baked isometric top-left directional.
```
