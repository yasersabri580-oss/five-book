# REFERENCE IMAGE PROMPT — WOODEN CRATE

---

## Purpose
This prompt generates the **canonical reference image** for the WoodenCrate.
All three damage states and the double-stack variant must be visually consistent with this reference.

---

## Reference Image Generation Prompt

```
2.5D isometric game asset — Wooden Crate (three damage states + double-stack variant) for the game IRON BREACH.

CAMERA: Isometric 2.5D, 30-degree pitch, south-east facing. Objects shown as standalone sprites on flat near-black background (#121212) or transparent. No environment.

ART STYLE: Stylized game asset. Crisp 2px black outline. Baked isometric lighting: top face is lightest, south face is mid-tone, east face is darkest. Wood grain visible on all planked faces. NOT pixel art. NOT photorealistic. Clean, readable, stylized — same style as all IRON BREACH environment objects.

REFERENCE SHEET: Four panels side by side.

PANEL 1 — INTACT CRATE:
- Cubic wooden crate, 0.8 isometric tiles wide × 0.8 deep × 0.8 tall.
- Wood planks visible on all faces: 3–4 horizontal planks per face, separated by thin dark gaps. Plank color: warm tan (#8A6A3A). Subtle wood grain texture on each plank — horizontal lines, slightly irregular.
- Four steel corner L-brackets: one at each vertical corner of the crate. Matte dark grey (#3A3A38). Simple flat L-shape bracket, 3 bolt holes visible per bracket.
- Lid on the top face: a plank-covered lid, same wood as sides. A small center hinge detail (dark metal bar across the center of the lid top face — suggesting it can be opened, though it is not interactive in gameplay).
- Small nails/bolts visible on the plank faces — 2–3 per plank, tiny dark dots (#2A1A08).
- One face (south-facing, visible to camera) has a worn military stencil: "FORGE ARMS CO." in a simple stencil font. The stencil is worn and faded — same color as the wood but darker (#5A3A18), slightly rough edges. The text is secondary detail, not the focal point.
- Overall the crate feels weathered and heavily used, not brand new.

PANEL 2 — DAMAGED CRATE (50% HP):
- Same crate, visibly damaged. Keep the same base shape and size.
- One plank on the south face is cracked diagonally — crack line splits the plank into two uneven halves. The crack gap has a slight dark void behind it.
- One corner L-bracket (top-left) is bent — the bracket arm that runs along the top edge is peeled away slightly, showing a small gap at the corner.
- The lid is ajar: the top face lid is angled 10–15° open — the near-camera corner is raised slightly. The hinge side is still down. You can see a dark interior gap where the lid is open.
- The stencil text is partially obscured by a scorch or impact mark on the south face — a dark smudge (#2A1A08) across part of the text.
- Overall shape still intact — it is recognizably the same crate, just clearly damaged.

PANEL 3 — DESTROYED DECAL (ground debris):
- The crate is gone. This is a flat ground-level debris sprite.
- Loose plank fragments: 3–4 individual plank pieces lying on the ground at irregular angles. Each is a short section of wood plank, same warm tan color, some cracked or splintered at the ends.
- One bent L-bracket in the debris — folded, sitting among the planks.
- A few small dark nail dots scattered around.
- The debris occupies roughly a 0.8 tile footprint — slightly spread.
- No height — this is a flat decal placed on the ground layer.

PANEL 4 — DOUBLE-STACK VARIANT:
- Two intact crates stacked vertically. Same 0.8 tile footprint, but 1.6 tiles tall.
- The top crate sits perfectly on the bottom crate. The stack is stable — no overhang. A slight shadow line at the join between the two crates.
- Top crate is identical to Panel 1. Bottom crate can have slightly more grime/dirt near the base.
- Both crates show their FORGE ARMS CO. stencil (only south-facing panels, one per crate).

COLOR PALETTE:
- Wood planks: #8A6A3A (base tan)
- Wood shadows/grain: #5A3A18 (darker streaks)
- Steel brackets: #3A3A38
- Nails: #2A1A08 (tiny dots)
- Stencil: #5A3A18 (faded, same family as wood shadow)
- Crack interior/lid gap: #1A0A04 (dark void)
- Outline: #0A0A0A (2px)

SCALE REFERENCE: Include a faint isometric tile grid ghost under each panel.

OUTPUT: One horizontal reference sheet, four panels: Intact, Damaged, Destroyed Decal, Double-Stack. All labeled. Dark background.
```
